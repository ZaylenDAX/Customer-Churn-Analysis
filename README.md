# 📊Customer-Churn-Analysis Using Power BI
Reducing Telecom customer churn through behavioral and revenue analytics.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company using Microsoft Power BI.
The goal of the analysis was to identify:

- ❓ Why customers churn
- ⚠️ Which customers are most likely to churn
- 💰 The financial impact of churn
- 🎯 Which customer segments require retention focus

The final solution was delivered through three interactive Power BI dashboards focused on:
- 📌 Executive Overview
- 👥 Customer Behavior
- 💵 Revenue Insights

---

# 🏢 Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based telecom businesses.
High churn rates lead to:
- 💸 Revenue loss
- 📉 Reduced customer lifetime value
- 📈 Increased acquisition costs
- ⚡ Lower profitability

This project helps the business understand the root causes of churn and identify actionable retention opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

The analysis aimed to:
- 📊 Measure customer churn rate
- 🔍 Identify major churn drivers
- 👥 Analyze customer behavior patterns
- 💰 Understand revenue impact

- 🧠 Recommend retention strategies

# 🔑 Key Findings

## ⚠️ Churn Rate Is Critically High

| Metric | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Total Customers | 6,677 |
| Churned Customers | 1,793 |
| Retained Customers | 4,884|
| Churn Rate | 26.85% |

The company experiences a critically high churn rate, indicating major retention issues.

## 📄 Contract Structure Strongly Influences Churn

| Contract Type | Churn Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Month-to-Month | 46.27% |
| One Year | 11.31% |
| Two Year | 2.79% |

✅ Long-term contracts significantly improve customer retention.

## ☎️ Customer Service Is a Major Churn Driver

- 🚨 Customers with 5 service calls show 100% churn
- 📞 Churned customers average 2.4 service calls
- ✅ Retained customers average only 0.37 calls

Support interactions serve as a strong churn warning signal.

## 🏆 Competitor Pressure Is Significant

The top churn reasons include:
- 💸 Competitor made better offer
- 📱 Competitor had better devices
- 📶 Competitor offered more data
  
This indicates strong competitive pressure within the telecom market.

## 💰 Revenue Impact Is Significant

| Metric | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Total Revenue | $7.24M |
| Revenue Lost | $1.36M |
| Revenue at Risk | $151K |

Customer churn represents a major revenue leakage issue.
